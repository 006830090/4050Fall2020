Safety Glasses

Wrap Around Style Safety Glasses

These glasses feature a stylish look and a polycarbonate lens with a pliable frame for comfort. They are available with either clear (Indoor), tinted (Outdoor), indoor/outdoor (Mirrored) or amber lenses. They are designed to provide protection from light to moderate impact hazards. They have an anti-Fog film, scratch resistant, shatter resistant, and meet and exceed the current ANSI Z87.1 standards (This specification number is stamped on the temple piece of each pair of glasses to prove they comply if an OSHA inspector shows up on the job sight)

"Trooper" Style Safety Glasses

Similar to the ones you see some of your favorite bike builders wearing, These glasses feature a specially coated polycarbonate foam padded lenses to provide maximum protection & scratch resistance. A wide temple makes them comfortable and stylish. They also feature soft nonslip rubber temple tips for added comfort & security. They will filter 99% of harmful uv rays. They are ANSI Z87.1-2010 certified and meet military MIL-PRF- 31013 CLAUSE 3.5.1.1 for high velocity impact protection. Available in clear, tinted, and mirrored.

First Aid Kits

#10 Man Plastic Case

This is a #10 industrial first aid kit that comes in an industrial plastic case (8" x 5" x 3 1⁄2”). The case features mounting brackets so it can be mounted in the office, at the job site, or on equipment. This kit satisfies the ANSI Z308.1-2003 and the federal OSHA standard 1910.151b for jobsite use.

#25 Man Metal Box

Made in USA. This is a #25 industrial first aid kit that comes in a metal case (11" x 8" x 3"). The case features mounting brackets so it can be mounted in the office, at the job site, or on equipment. This kit satisfies the ANSI 2308.1-2003 and the federal OSHA standard 1910.151b for jobsite use. This has been the standard in the construction industry for years.

#50 Man Metal Box

Made in USA. This is a weatherproof kit that is packaged in a 20 gauge steel case, with a gasket to protect against water and dust. They are rated for 50 people or less and are perfect for industry, office, spas, camps, food service and team sports.

Fire Extinguishers

Buckeye American Made Fire Extinguishers

Made in USA by Buckeye Fire Equipment. This is absolutely the finest fire extinguisher money can buy. They are U.L., D.O.T., and U.S. Coast Guard approved. They are a high performance dry chemical extinguisher that features a hot dipped galvanized cylinder with a metal head. The valve parts and all fittings are heavy duty chrome plated brass or stainless steel. The 2-1/2 lb and 5 lb units come with metal vehicle strap brackets, and the 10 lb unit comes with a wall mount bracket. This product can only be sold in the continental 48 as it is considered hazmat for air shipping. No Alaska, Hawaii, etc. Available in 2.5, 5, and 10 lb sizes but are subject to availability.
